A Sons Exclusives

Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh

About the Shop

A Sons Exclusives is a premium watch store in Ghaziabad, established in 2014. Known for their wide selection of timepieces, they also take pride in being environmentally responsible. As a part of the Battery Bin network, they safely dispose of used batteries from watches and welcome drop-offs from the community.

Our bin is placed on one of their counters along with an awareness poster, making it easy for customers and locals to participate in safe battery recycling.
