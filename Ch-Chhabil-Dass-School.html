About the School

Ch. Chhabil Dass School, located in Wave City, Ghaziabad, is committed to building a generation of environmentally responsible students. The school integrates sustainability into everyday learning and actively supports eco-friendly practices across its campus.

As a proud Battery Bin partner, the school hosts a battery collection point that encourages students, staff, and parents to safely dispose of used batteries, reducing harm to the environment.
