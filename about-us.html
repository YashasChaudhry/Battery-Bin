Our Story

Battery Bin began with a simple moment of awareness — I was about to throw away a used battery when I paused and wondered just how harmful it might be. That small hesitation led me to uncover the massive environmental impact of e-waste, especially when batteries are discarded improperly.

The idea stayed with me until I finally decided to act. At first, I had doubts. I had never led anything like this before, and I wasn’t sure if anyone would support it — or if I’d even find a recycling partner. After countless emails, one company responded. That single reply gave me the push to begin.

Today, Battery Bin is a student-led initiative working with schools, businesses, and NGOs across Ghaziabad and parts of Noida. We make battery disposal simple and responsible — keeping harmful chemicals out of landfills and protecting the environment.

This is just the beginning. Our goal is to expand across Delhi NCR and build a culture where safe battery disposal is second nature.

Our Mission

Battery Bin is a youth-led initiative on a mission to make battery recycling easy, accessible, and meaningful for everyone — from homes and schools to local shops and offices.

With active collection points across Ghaziabad, Noida, and plans to expand across Delhi NCR, we work to keep toxic battery waste out of landfills and our environment.

We're not driven by profit — we're powered by purpose. Backed by trusted recycling partners and NGOs, we exist to protect the planet, one battery at a time.

Our Impact

Small steps lead to big change — and Battery Bin is proof of that.

We've already prevented hundreds of harmful batteries from polluting landfills and soil, thanks to our growing network of dedicated collection points.

  • 🏫 Active in schools and shops across Ghaziabad and Noida
  • ♻️ Collaborating with certified recyclers and a local NGO to ensure safe disposal
  • 📢 Reached over 5,000+ individuals with our message of safe recycling

We're just getting started — with your help, we're building a cleaner, safer future, one battery at a time.
