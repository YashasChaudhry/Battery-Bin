What is Battery Bin?

Battery Bin is a grassroots eco-friendly initiative dedicated to collecting and safely recycling used batteries from schools, homes, and businesses. By preventing harmful chemicals from entering the environment, we promote sustainability and community awareness.

Why It Matters

Batteries contain toxic metals that can pollute water and soil. Recycling even one AA battery prevents contamination of up to 100 liters of water. Small actions make a big difference!

  • ✔️ Every recycled battery keeps heavy metals out of landfills
  • ✔️ Supports circular economy and resource reuse
  • ✔️ Protects local water, soil, and wildlife
